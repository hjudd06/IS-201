Instructions

  1. Grab Ingredients
    • 1 cup Natural Sourdough Starter
    • 6 Cups Flour
    • 2 Cups Water
    • 2 tsp. Salt
    • 1/4 tsp. Yeast
  2. Mix all ingredients in a kitchen aid or similar mixer
  3. Mix for about 5 minutes until the dough is only slightly sticky
  4. After mixing, place dough into a greased glass bowl and cover
  5. Let rest for 5-10 hours
  6. After resting, cut the dough in half and shape into 2 loaves on a floured work surface
  7. Place the loaves onto parchment paper and let rest another 30 minutes
  8. After 30 minutes, place the loaves into dutch ovens and cook at 450 degrees for 20 minutes with the lid on
  9. Cook another 15 minutes with the lid off
